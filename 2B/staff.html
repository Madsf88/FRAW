About

ITU.film is a film club situated at The IT-University of Copenhagen. We are a handful of people who are passionate about film, and we want to bring our passion to the students and faculty of ITU in a fun and friendly atmosphere. This is why we arrange movie screenings about once every month in the student caf√© Analog, and we would love to see you there! So bring your classmates and join us for an awesome flick and free popcorn at the next screening. It is completely free for all students and faculty.

Follow our Facebook page and get all the announcements from upcoming events on our Facebook page. Or participate in the debate in our Facebook group.
